About Me

I strive to live intentionally, ensuring that my thoughts and actions aim to improve the standards of living for myself, others, and all life on Earth. While this may seem daunting or fantastical at first glance, I adopt a modest approach, doing what I can no matter how small, as long as I'm aiming upward. I achieve this through an incremental yet disciplined cycle of rethinking and relearning, while trusting scientific consensus. I embrace criticism and mistakes as part of the growth process. I don't claim to know it all, but I learn from my mistakes and try again.
